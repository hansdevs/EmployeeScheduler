Our Story

The Scheduler was founded in 2020 by a team of software engineers and business operations experts who were frustrated with the limitations of existing scheduling tools. We set out to create a solution that would be powerful enough for large enterprises but intuitive enough for small businesses.

What started as a simple scheduling tool has evolved into a comprehensive workforce management platform trusted by over 5,000 businesses worldwide, from small local shops to multinational corporations.
